AiiDA virtual hands-on tutorial in 2021 Spring¶

2 days AiiDA hands-on tutorial in April or May will be held as a virtual zoom meeting.

Plan¶

This is a training session to feel AiiDA (http://www.aiida.net/). People who want to join will get together by themselves. No fee is required to join. At this time, the number of participants will have to be kept minimum because we don’t have enough people to support participants.

In the hands-on tutorial, we will basically follow the previous AiiDA tutorial material (we will modify this material a little). Participants are requested to install virtualbox to setup Quantum Mobile on their PCs and the hands-on tutorials will be run on the PCs.

Date & Location¶

2 days AiiDA tutorial workshop in April or May will be held as a virtual zoom meeting.

Schedule¶

In principle, no rigorous schedule is set-up. Participants will work on the tutorial or what they want to do in their own paces. Questions and discussions will be raised accordingly.

First day 9:00 - 17:00¶

  • 9:00 - 9:10: Opening remarks

  • 9:10 - 9:30: Introduction to AiiDA

  • 9:30 - 12:00: Getting started

  • 13:00 - Afternoon: Verdi command line, Verdi shell and AiiDA objects, Submit, monitor and debug calculations

Second day 9:00 - 17:00¶

  • 9:00 - 9:30: Presentation “Running phonon calculation with an iterative algorithm by AiiDA, AiiDA-VASP, and AiiDA-phonopy”

  • 9:30 - 12:00: Queries in AiiDA and Workflows

  • 13:00 - Afternoon: Queries in AiiDA and Workflows, and maybe more (e.g., setup AiiDA on one’s computer, AiiDA-VASP)

  • 16:50 - 17:00: Closing

Preparation of AiiDA environment on your computer¶

Most participants are recommended to prepare Quantum Mobile on their PCs. Quantum Mobile contains not only AiiDA daemon but also the open source software packages (e.g., Quantum Espresso) that are required to run the tutorial materials.

  • Quantum Mobile setup

Advanced participants may prepare AiiDA environment on their system by themselves.

  • https://aiida.readthedocs.io/projects/aiida-core/en/latest/intro/get_started.html

For VASP users, AiiDA-VASP can be installed following this documentation. AiiDA-VASP (the AiiDA plugin for VASP) itself is an open source code, but VASP is not. Only the participants who have the valid license can run the VASP code on their computers.

  • https://aiida-vasp.readthedocs.io/en/latest/
